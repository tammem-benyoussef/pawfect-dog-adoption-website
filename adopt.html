Before you apply

What we look for

We want every adoption to be a lifelong success.

🏠

Stable Living Situation

A secure, pet-friendly home suited to the dog's size and energy level.

⏰

Time & Commitment

Dogs need daily walks, feeding, and companionship — every single day.

💰

Financial Readiness

Food, vet care, and supplies are ongoing costs. We want to ensure you're prepared.

👨‍👩‍👧

Household Agreement

Everyone in the home — including kids and other pets — should be on board.

📋

18+ Years Old

Valid ID required for verification during the meet-and-greet.
